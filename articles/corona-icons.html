These icons appear now on my site to please you to keep distance, stay home, wash your hands and wear a mask.

If you want to add them too, just add this to your head:
<link href="https://bimi124.github.io/fontawesome/css/all.css" rel="stylesheet"/><link href="https://bimi124.github.io/css/coronavirus.css" rel="stylesheet"/>
And this to your body:
<div id="covid-19-msg"><icon class="fas fa-people-arrows"/><icon class="fas fa-house-user"/><icon class="fas fa-hands-wash"/><icon class="fas fa-head-side-mask"/></div>
